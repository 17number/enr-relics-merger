About this tool (click to expand)
  • Combine relic effect images of Relics (slots 1–3) and Deep of Night Depth Relics (slots 4–6) into a single image. (Examples)
  • Select two images from the Relic Rites, Status, or Preset screens.
  • You can also drag & drop or paste images directly (Win: Ctrl+V / Mac: ⌘+V).
  • For console users: After capturing screenshots on PS5 or Xbox, you can transfer them to your PC or smartphone. (PS5 → PS App / Xbox → Xbox App)
  • It would be great if you could share your combined image on X or Discord.
Notes (Click to Expand)
  • Each image must show the relic effects for slots 1–3 and 4–6.
  • Due to the cropping method used, images may not combine correctly depending on their size and other factors.
  • You can select up to 2 images. If you select more than 2 images via drag & drop, only the first 2 will be used.
  • The selected images are processed in the browser and are not sent to the server.
  • This tool is unofficial and is not affiliated with ELDEN RING NIGHTREIGN.
  • If you have any questions, please contact us via Issues. We will respond as much as possible. It would be helpful if you could check for similar issues beforehand.
